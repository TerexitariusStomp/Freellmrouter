Implement weighted scoring with at least:
- credit/quota remaining
- task capability fit
- latency or RPM
- health status
- policy compatibility

Use a formula like:
```text
score =
  credit_weight * normalized_credit_remaining +
  capability_weight * task_fit_score +
  speed_weight * normalized_speed +
```
